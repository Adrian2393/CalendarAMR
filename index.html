About

The Custom Calendar Project is a unique time management tool designed with a bold army theme. It spans from May 13 to September 23, 2025, helping users track daily progress. This project was inspired by the need for disciplined planning and visual feedback. Each day that passes is marked with a red "X", bringing a sense of accomplishment and accountability.

Features

  • Army-themed interface for motivation and structure
  • Daily automatic updates that cross out past days
  • Progress bar to visualize time elapsed
  • Starts on Monday for better weekly alignment

How It Works

The calendar dynamically displays each day from May 13 to September 23. Past days are marked with a red "X" and styled in red. A progress bar shows how far along you are in the 134-day journey. You can use this calendar to track goals, training, or countdowns.
